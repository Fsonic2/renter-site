Welcome to the Cat Rental Store

Thompson Machinery provides access to a broad selection of rental equipment, related products and services to a diverse range of industries. It’s our focus to deliver the flexible, cost-effective rental solution necessary to help your business increase productivity while reducing owning and operating expenses.

"I always recommend Thompson Machinery because of their service, because of their attitude, because of their good equipment that we receive when I've rented equipment."
—Brian Schmidt, C.O. Christian and Sons CO., INC.

We carry one of the largest rental fleets in the country including low-hour, meticulously maintained articulated trucks, skid steer loaders, hydraulic excavators and virtually any other type of compact or heavy equipment. In addition to our selection of both Cat® and Allied manufactured machines, we also offer an extensive inventory of rental power systems and site support equipment ranging from backup and primary generator sets, temperature control and air compressors to lift trucks, aerial work platforms and work tool attachments.

As an authorized Cat dealership for Western and Central Tennessee and Northern Mississippi, we offer the productive, durable and reliable Cat rental equipment you are looking for backed by expert, around-the-clock support and peerless customer service. From on-site preventive maintenance and emergency repairs to flexible short- and long-term rentals, competitive pricing and financial services, we’re the one-stop shop for all your needs.

Learn more about our rentals products and services by giving us a call, get in touch online or visiting one of our convenient equipment rental locations in Tennessee and Mississippi today.
